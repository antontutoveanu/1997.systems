Intended as an intermittent diet replacement.

Excretions from the liver and gallbladder are largely re-absorbed in the small intestine with ~5% passing to the large intestine as usual digestive waste.

Solid fibrous meals on 1-2 "detox days" per week is recommended to clear the leftover bile from the gut despite a no-residue diet.

Based on the traditional work week.
